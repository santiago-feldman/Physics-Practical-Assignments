# Physics Laboratory and Simulation Assignments (Classical Mechanics, Electromagnetism, Quantum Mechanics, Solid State Physics) | ITBA

This repository contains the practical assignments done for the following subjects at Instituto Tecnológico de Buenos Aires:

- 93.41 Physics I (Classical Mechanics)
- 93.43 Physics III (Electromagnetism)
- 93.47 Physics IV (Special Relativity, Quantum Mechanics, Statistical Mechanics, Nuclear Physics)
- 23.09 Electronic Physics (Solid-state Physics, Semiconductor Physics and Devices)

## Physics I 
1) Kinematics, Dynamics, and Uncertainty Analysis
2) Rigid Body and Rototranslational Motion
## Physics III
1) Characteristic Curves
2) DC Circuits
3) Charging and Discharging of a Capacitor
4) AC Circuits
## Physics IV
1) Millikan Experiment
2) Determination of the Specific Charge of the Electron
3) Franck–Hertz Experiment
4) Gas chromatography–mass spectrometry (GC–MS)
5) Determination of Planck's Constant
## Electronic Physics
1) Study of Semiconductor Devices and Early effect in Junction Field-Effect Transistors (JFET)
